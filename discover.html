Rural Life

Humble and simple can be the best way to describe the life around Taalish. One can go and explore the villages surrounding. You'll be surprised to see the simple way they lead their lives. Mud houses with small yards, basic cloths and simple food with long walks keep them healthy, fresh and smiling. Experience the way they live and earn their livelihood yet they are happy. Joy in simplicity is what one can take with them.

Hatkeshwar (Natural Bridge)

Human minds can only be surprised. All we can do is just imagine and nature does it all. Experience the thrill of walking on a bridge that's imagines, architectured and constructed by nature alone. Walk on the Hatkeshwar Bridge a fascinating natural path bridging to mountains.

Ancient Treasure

Don't get wrong ideas about the treasure here, yet it's invaluable. Taalish sits near the ancient path to enter into the Konkan region. This used to be a gateway on to the ghats and people used to carry the goods through this passage. This spot is still here which used to be a pitstop in ancient times. The travellers used to stop, eat and rest. Be thrilled by walking on the same path as our ancestors did once. You can still find the scriptures on the walls of these caves describing about the place.
