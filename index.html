The Talish Resort

Welcome to the world of Taalish where adrenaline meets tranquility.

Taalish sits daringly at a cliff of scenic location of Nane Ghat providing a perfect gateway from the demanding world. Laced with all the comforts of a modern day, the resort is adorned with rustic charm of the soil. Right from its structure, amenities, facilities and food.

Overlooking in the deep valley where roaring waterfalls attempt to jump down and then gushing wind forces them on the cliff, Taalish is an ideal destination to seek solace.

Passage To Tranquility
