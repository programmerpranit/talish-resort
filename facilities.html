Foot Trails And Treks

Going to the nature makes you more grounded. It makes you go back to the basics as far as possible. And when you are at a place like Taalish where nature has its own home, all you can do is to use your feet to wander. Explore the beauty of this unending plateau, dense forest, roaring waterfalls, challenging forts and much more fun on your foot when you are here. We're sure that it's going to be an experience you would love to grab more.

Traditional Indian Games

India has a rich tradition of sport and most of them are played in the mud. What’s more fun to enjoy these games at a location which itself is made of soil? Experience the Indian games like Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Lagori, Surparambya, Aatyapaatya, Viti-Dandu and much more at Taalish.

Village Safari in a tractor/jeep

Taalish is nestled in the picturesque locals of Junnar near Nane Ghat. Obviously, the you have many neighbouring villages in the surrounding. Explore them in a way the villagers would do. Take a safari that takes you through these rustic roads in a Tractor or a Jeep. It's going to be fun to enjoy this bumpy ride by experiencing what has lost.

Village walks

We arrange village walks if you want to explore the rural life of India even more closely. Walk through the mud houses, paddy fields, dirt tracks, local artisans and overall lifestyle the villagers live. It’s fun to watch a different life that’s way beyond our imaginations. They just leave us in awe by their humility and simplicity. Tired of walking? Take a bicycle instead. Explore the fun of mountain biking.

Rock climbing, Rappelling, Camping

It's not only about the natural beauty at Taalish. It' a place where you feed the most for your adrenaline rush. The surroundings of the place throws many challenges at you. The valley is so deep and full of gushing wind force that even the falling water defies gravity. Imagine doing rappelling in such valley or conquering the rocky mountains. Set up your tent and camp at night on this plateau in the company of abundant stars, cool breezes and your wild imaginations. It’s a truly inspiring atmosphere where anyone would become a creative.

Bonfire

You are in the nature where you look up and all you can see is an uninterrupted view of zillions of twinkling stars, where cool breezes caress you and you can sit for hours by listening to the melody of tranquility. Well, this is the right time for a bonfire with friends and family. Conversations can go on and on over cups of coffee and a guitar around a warm fire. Seriously, life can't get happier!
